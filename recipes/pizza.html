Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • One 12 inch baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • One 2oz can of black olives
  • 1/2 small red onion, chopped
  • One 4oz can of artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Pre heat the oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Spread pesto on the pizza crust. Top with tomato, bell pepper, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts and feta cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and browned, about 8 to 10 minutes.
